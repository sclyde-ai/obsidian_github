
		average price
		- arithmetic average
			$$ 
			A_T = ½ ∫_0^T S_t dt
			$$ 
		- geometric average
			$$ 
			A_T = ½ ∫_0^T ∬(S_t) dt
			$$ 
		- average price option
			- call
				$$ 
				V_T = (A_T - K)^+
				$$ 
			- put
				$$ 
				V_T = (K-A_T)^+
				$$ 
		- average strike option
			- call
				$$ 
				V_T = (S_T - k A_T)^+
				$$ 
			- put
				$$ 
				V_T = (k A_T - S_T)^+
				$$
