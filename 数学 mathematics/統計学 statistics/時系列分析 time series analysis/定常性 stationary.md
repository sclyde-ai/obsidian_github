
    時間的な不変性をもつ
    - 弱定常性 weak stationary
        - 期待値一定 the expectation is constant
            $$
            \mathbb E[y_t] = \mu 
            $$
        - 共分散が時間差に依存 the covariance depends on only the time span
            $$
            cov(y_t, y_{t-k}) = \mathbb E[(y_t-\mu)(y_{t-k}-\mu)] = \gamma_k
            $$
