- topological space (X, O)
$$
 \exists \mathcal O_x, \mathcal O_y \subset \mathcal O, \mathcal O_x \cup \mathcal O_y = \phi \\ (x \in \mathcal O_x \land y \notin \mathcal O_x)
\land
(x \notin \mathcal O_y \land y \in \mathcal O_y)
$$
