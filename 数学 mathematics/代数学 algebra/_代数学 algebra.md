- 解の公式
            $$
            a x^2 + b x + c = 0 \\
             x^2 + \frac{b}{a} x + \frac{c}{a} = 0 \\
            (x - \frac{b}{2a})^2 - \frac{b^2}{4a^2} + \frac{c}{a} = 0 \\
            (x - \frac{b}{2a})^2 = \frac{b^2}{4a^2} - \frac{c}{a}  \\
            (x - \frac{b}{2a})^2 = \frac{b^2 -4ac}{4a^2} \\
            x = \frac{b}{2a} \pm \sqrt{\frac{b^2 -4ac}{4a^2}} \\
            x = \frac{b\pm \sqrt{b^2 -4ac}}{2a} 
            $$
