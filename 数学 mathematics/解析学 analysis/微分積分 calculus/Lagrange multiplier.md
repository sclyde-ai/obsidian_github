- C^1 級関数 f(x, y) g(x, y)
- 条件1
    - 判別式 $D_f(a, b)$
    $$ g(x, y)=0 \mathbb Rightarrow D_f(a, b)>0 $$
- 条件2
    $$ g_x(a, b)\ne 0 \land g_y(a, b) \ne 0 $$
$$ \existss k \in \mathbb R, F_x(a, b, k) = F_y(a, b, k) = F_\lambda(a, b, k) = 0 \\ F(x, y, \lambda) = f(x, y) - \lambda g(x, y) $$
- how to use
    制約条件gの下でのfの最大/最小化
    $$ \max / \min f(x, y)\ s.t.\ g(x, y) = 0 $$
