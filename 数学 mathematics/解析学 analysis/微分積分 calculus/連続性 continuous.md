$$ \lim_{x \to a} f(x) = f(a) $$
$$ \forall \delta>0, \exists \epsilon >0, \forall x \in \mathbb R \\ 0 < |x-a|< \delta \Rightarrow |f(x)-\alpha| < \epsilon $$
- 左連続 left-hand
    $$ a \in X \\ \lim_{x \to a+} f(x) \in \mathbb R\\ \lim_{x \to a-} f(x) = f(a) $$
    $$ \forall \delta>0, \exists \epsilon >0, \forall x \in \mathbb R \\ 0 < |x-a|< \delta \Rightarrow |f(x)-\alpha| < \epsilon $$
- 右連続 right-hand
    $$ a \in X \\ \lim_{x \to a+} f(x) \in \mathbb R\\ \lim_{x \to a+} f(x) = f(a) $$
    $$ \forall \delta>0, \exists \epsilon >0, \forall x \in \mathbb R \\ 0 < |x-a|< \delta \Rightarrow |f(x)-\alpha| < \epsilon $$
