                $$
                V_{Early\ Exercise} = A\left(\frac{S}{S^*}\right)^\lambda
                $$
                $$
                A = \frac{S}{\lambda}(1-e^{(c-r)t}\Phi(d_1^*)) \\
                \lambda = \frac{1}{2}\left(-(\beta-1) + \sqrt{(\beta-1)^2 + \frac{4\alpha}{h}}\right)
                $$
                $$
                \alpha = \frac{2r}{\sigma^2} \\
                \beta = \frac{2(r-\delta)}{\sigma^2}
                $$
                $$
                \tau = T-t \\
                h(\tau) = 1-e^{-r\tau}
                $$
